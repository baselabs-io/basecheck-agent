# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do not report suspected vulnerabilities in a public issue. Email
[info@basecheck.ai](mailto:info@basecheck.ai) with a clear description,
affected version, reproduction steps, and any relevant logs or proof of
concept.

We will acknowledge the report, assess the impact, and coordinate a fix and
disclosure with you.

## Other reports and communication

For installation problems, incorrect or noisy findings, SIEM delivery issues,
feature requests, and general communication, email
[info@basecheck.ai](mailto:info@basecheck.ai).
